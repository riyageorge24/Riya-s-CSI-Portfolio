Internship Montage

Project 1: Anti-Bow Cassette Stand

Mill: Galv 2

Mentor: Justin Speer

Description: Designed a stand for the Anti-Bow Cassettes, enhancing safety and operational efficiency. Performed NASTRAN analysis to validate load-bearing capacity and strategically utilized existing inventory for material selection.

Project 2: Furnace Structural Repair

Mill: HSF

Mentor: Curtis Coombs

Description: Assessed the structural integrity of the furnace wall, calculated and simulated counterweight requirements, lifting distance from counterweight, and sourcing counterweights. Collaborated with riggers to prepare for and execute the installation of a 60 ft I-beam.

Project 3: FEA Analysis Crane Hook

Mill: Galv 2

Mentor: Justin Speer

Description: Modeled and conducted NASTRAN on a crane hook to evaluate its structural capacity and ensure it can safely handle a specified load of 6000 pounds.

Project 4: Remodeling Lifting Beam

Mill: HSF

Mentor: Justin Speer, Curtis Coombs & Pedro

Description: Modeled a lifting beam, including ultrasonic testing and NASTRAN analysis, to validate structural integrity.

Project 5: Floor Repair Work

Mill: HSF

Mentor: Curtis Coombs

Description: Executed repairs on the mill floor, focusing on restoring structural stability and ensuring safe working conditions.

Project 6: Debander Robot Stairway and Platform

Mill: HSF

Mentor: Curtis Coombs

Description: Designed a plan view for a new staircase and platform for the Debander Robot, including structural calculations and compliance with relevant codes.

Project 7 Baffle Sensor Cover

Mill: Galv 2

Description: Modeled a cover for the baffle sensor to protect it from environmental damage and ensure accurate readings during operations.

Mentor: Justin Speer
